1/8

Brief & 'Hello' Call

24 hours

Firstly, fill in the brief. Our designer want to understand the task in all details and know all the answears. Or just send a request on any social media you can see below (click on it). Within 24 hours our Sales Manager will contact with you and you will ask whatever you want :)

Instagram Gmail Facebook LinkedIn

2/8

Competitors Analysis

6 hours

After finding out all details about your company, our team is going to research competitors too. In this way we will understand how to make an outstanding website that will be attached to people.

3/8

Wireframing

6-12 hours (depends on project)

On this stage a UX/UI designer will create a structure for the project. After that, we will waiting for your feedback. If there are some changes - we will implement them.

4/8

UI Concept

6 hours

And now you have seen the principles how will work the website. The next step - show how it will be looking like. To do that - the designer selects one (or several) pieces from the project and visualize the UI.

5/8

UI Implementation

6-10 hours (depends on project)

After you approve the concept, we will design the rest of the product's screens. During the process we create unique icons, illustations and logo.

6/8

Codding...

16 hours on averege

Now after confirmation a prototype by you our Devs start to develop your product.

7/8

Testing...

16 hours in averege

At this stage, we will create and test your product and will repeat that until the product became Ideal!

8/8

Final Eyes

16 hours in averege

Here you check our results and give us a feedback.
